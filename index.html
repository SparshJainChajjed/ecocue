Understand and Reduce Your Organization’s Carbon Footprint

Most organizations want to reduce emissions, but data lives in silos across electricity bills, transport logs, waste records and suppliers. CarbonCue unifies this data into a single dashboard, calculates emissions using standard EPA/IPCC factors and gives clear cues on where and how to act.

Upload Carbon Data

Upload a CSV file containing your organization’s activity data. Each row should contain Date (YYYY‑MM‑DD), Department, Category (e.g., electricity, transport_car, transport_bus, transport_train, transport_flight, waste), Unit (kWh, km, kg), and Amount. You can also load a sample dataset to explore the dashboard.
